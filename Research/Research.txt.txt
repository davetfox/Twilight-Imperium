## FAQ and memory aids

**Does ÔÇťwhenÔÇŁ beat ÔÇťafterÔÇŁ?** Yes. ÔÇťWhenÔÇŁ happens at the moment of the event and takes priority over ÔÇťafter.ÔÇŁ If two cards appear to compete, start there. ţłÇciteţłéturn30view1ţłéturn26view2ţłü
